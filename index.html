07 — Colour System Rules
60
Dominant
60% Obsidian or Architectural White. These two never share equal weight in a single composition.
30
Secondary
30% drawn from Greige or its tonal scale. Acts as the warm mediator between black and white planes.
10
Tonal Detail
10% reserved for mid-tonal variants — Silver, Fog, Stone, and Linen — used for typography, borders, and depth.
0
Never
No colours outside this system. No warm or cool colour introductions without a full brand review.
